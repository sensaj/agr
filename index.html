When our people grow, our clients grow: As our people grow, we learn how to deliver better solutions to problems – some of which our clients don’t know they have. What We Do

ENERGY

GIS has partnered with renewable energy and electrification development and consulting firm with a focus in the public sector. Our work aims to bring clean, renewable, reliable electricity to our customers and the grid.

AVIATION

To provide luxurious, comforting and customized solutions to our clients. GIS has partnered with SONNIG International which owns private jet. GIS has planned the best unique services that is to provide private jets, helicopters for individual as well as business preferences. Along with that we will take care of your business partners, guests and family members in order to make the journey successful.

MINING

To provide mining and associated construction services of the highest professional level with a safety and environmental record second to none. GIS focuses on working with industry OEMs and Specilist companies to consolidate and provide a single supplier focal point.

TRADING

This division is being envisaged to trade in commodities like Cereals and grains copper, coffee, cocoa, iron ore and all kind of construction materials etc all over the world.

Oil & Gas Trading

This division will be setting up a trading hub under the company. Using the Chairman’s experience.

INFRASTRUCTURE

We are committed to providing the best possible construction expertise & service to ensure cost effective and successful projects. We work closely with our clients to accurately interpret their dreams/visions in drawings and bring them to the desired reality through construction solutions to meet their needs; We communicate with our clients throughout the construction process to keep them informed of progress and to ensure that our project keeps on schedule and within budget.

SEA PORT SERVICE AND EQUIPMENT

GIS has partnered with port handling equipment and suppliers to ensure EFFICIENCY as it is the decisive factor when handling cargo at both seaports and inland harbours. Loading and unloading times have to be kept to a minimum. Flexible logistics and prevailing value for money are what make port facilities competitive on the international scene. Latest technologies and high-quality standards guarantee maximum performance and equipment availability.
